Emergency Numbers

In Cyprus, dial 112 for any major emergency. This connects to police, ambulance, or fire services across the EU.

  • General emergency (EU standard): 112
  • Police (island-wide backup number): 199
  • Ambulance / Medical: 199
  • Fire service: 199
